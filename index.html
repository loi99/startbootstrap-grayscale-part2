Salar de Uyuni

Salar de Uyuni is the worlds largest salt flat, "measuring 12,106sq Km and sits at 11,984ft". The unimaginable mirror view forms when there's a little water on the salt surface, matched with the pure white salt flat underneath creates a surreal reflection of the blue sky.
